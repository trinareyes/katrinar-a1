Patented Technology

Brainfm holds patens on key processes for creating functional music, including technology to elicit strong neural phase locking—allowing populations of neurons to engage in various kinds of coordinated activity—and technology to remove distraction in sound.

This makes our music unique, purpose-built to steer you into a desired mental state. In otherwords, we've found new ways to create music that helps you do what you need to do.
